LA SIERRITA

RESIDENCE

Lake|Flato Architects
2018 - 2020

introduction

La Sierrita Residence is a private vacation house located among the beautiful hills in Monterrey, Mexico. The Material palette is designed to embrace the vernacular building language and local artisan products. The bold formed concrete is balanced with weathered wood siding, to deliver the clean and sophisticated look with warmth and comfort.

As the two concrete volumes intersecting, the open great room for gathering and the private living areas are naturally connected and divided.

I joined the project for construction documents and I mainly participated in the interior design. I helped product research and created design options for great room and living space, with furniture layouts, delivered options with rendering and technical drawings. I was heavily involved in finalizing the documentation in Revit, detailing door and windows, fireplaces.

on the hill

Located on a steep hill created interesting design challenges and opportunities. As driving down the main road gives a clear view of the house, landscape is strategically designed to provide a level of privacy; the roof wi ll be covered with gravel and par t of the roof will be designed as green roof. The concept promoted here is to let nature take over, reclaiming the house and adding a new layer of “design“ on top of the built structure.

site plan

As the site plan shows, La Sierrita embraced the existing landscape, not only by full sized window and doors that introduced visual connection, by also by the porches and private courtyard space that encouraged outdoor activities.
